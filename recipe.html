Pilau Recipe

This recipe for authentic Kenyan-style Beef Pilau combines tender, spiced beef with rice for a savory, aromatic dish, usually served with kachumbari (a fresh onion and tomato salsa).

Preparation Time: 20 mins | Cooking Time: 40-50 mins | Serves 4-5

Ingredients

  • Meat & Stock: 500g-1kg beef (or goat), cut into small, equal cubes
  • Rice: 750g (approx. 3 cups) basmati or pishori rice, washed

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Meat:Boil the beef cubes with a little garlic, ginger, and salt until tender. Keep the remaining broth.
